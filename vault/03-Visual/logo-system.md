> Last updated: March 26, 2026

## Visual Identity Key Takeaway

The StartSuite visual identity is modern, dark-mode-first, and anchored by a vibrant purple gradient. The bidirectional arrow icon is the most distinctive element — it communicates exchange, momentum, and the compounding value of the StartSuite model in a single mark.

---

## Icon

- **Description:** Stylized letter 'S' formed by two counter-rotating arrows — one pointing left (upper), one pointing right (lower). Suggests exchange, momentum, and bidirectional movement.
- **Gradient:** Deep purple (#6607E1) to light purple (#BC98FF) — diagonal gradient from bottom-left to top-right
- **Symbolism:** The opposing arrows represent the exchange between StartSuite and its clients — strategy in, brand out. The S-form grounds it in the company name.

## Usage Guidelines

1. Always maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the 'S' icon
2. Never stretch, rotate, or distort the logo
3. Never apply the logo in colors other than the approved palette
4. Never recreate the logo — always use the provided files
5. Minimum size: 120px wide for horizontal, 80px wide for stacked icon-only
6. On photography, ensure sufficient contrast — use the reversed version on dark images

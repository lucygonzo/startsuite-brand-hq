---

## Typography Rules

1. Use Plus Jakarta Sans exclusively — no mixing with other typefaces
2. Headlines should be Bold or ExtraBold — never Regular weight for display sizes
3. All-caps labels use SemiBold with `0.08em` letter-spacing
4. Body copy line-height: `1.6` for readability
5. Do not use italic — the brand voice is direct and confident, not hesitant

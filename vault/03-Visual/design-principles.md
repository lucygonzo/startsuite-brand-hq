# StartSuite Design Principles

> Last updated: March 26, 2026
> Source: `brandData.ts` — `visualIdentity.designPrinciples`

---

## 1. Purposeful Gradient

The purple gradient is the brand's most distinctive asset. Use it with intention — for the logo, primary CTAs, and hero moments. Overuse dilutes its impact.

---

## 2. Dark Mode First

The StartSuite website and brand materials default to dark mode. Light mode is a secondary application. When in doubt, go dark.

---

## 3. Rounded, Not Sharp

The logo, wordmark, and UI elements all use rounded corners and smooth curves. Avoid sharp angles — they conflict with the brand's approachable, modern tone.

---

## 4. White Space as Premium Signal

Generous white space signals premium quality. Resist the urge to fill every pixel. Let the brand breathe.

---

## 5. Motion as Momentum

The brand is about speed and momentum. Subtle animations — scrolling tickers, smooth transitions, entrance animations — reinforce this without being distracting.

## Overview

Visible is an investor updates and fundraising CRM platform. Founders use it to send structured updates to their investors, track fundraising pipelines, and manage investor relationships. The product sits at the intersection of founder communication and capital management — a narrow but important wedge in the startup stack.

They've raised $3M+ and serve primarily seed-to-Series B founders who are actively raising or managing investor relationships. The product is straightforward: templates for investor updates, metrics dashboards, and a CRM for tracking investor conversations.

## Why They Matter to StartSuite

Visible serves founders in the active fundraising phase — exactly when those founders also need polished brand assets, pitch decks, and investor-facing narratives. A founder using Visible to send monthly updates would benefit from StartSuite-produced brand materials included in those updates. The overlap is in the "founder who takes communication seriously" persona, which is StartSuite's core early adopter.

## Key Numbers

- Raised: $3M+
- Pricing: Free to $150/mo
- Focus: Seed through Series B founders
- GTM: Content marketing, VC partnerships

## What StartSuite Can Learn

1. **Investor communication is a brand moment.** Every monthly update a founder sends through Visible is a brand touchpoint. StartSuite could create investor update templates or brand-consistent reporting formats as an add-on — turning a communication task into a brand reinforcement opportunity.
2. **VC partnerships as distribution.** Visible partners with VCs who recommend it to portfolio companies. StartSuite should study this channel — if a VC recommends StartSuite to all portfolio companies at onboarding, that's a scalable acquisition channel.

## Positioning & Language Worth Noting

- "Raise capital, engage investors, and leverage your key startup metrics" — functional, benefit-driven
- They position investor updates as a strategic activity, not a chore — same reframing StartSuite does with brand management

## What StartSuite Can Learn

- **The pivot is the lesson.** Tome was well-funded, had strong brand awareness, and was an early mover in AI presentations. They still couldn't compete with Gamma's scale and product-led growth. This validates that the presentation tool market is winner-take-most, and the winner is likely Gamma.
- **Selling to AngelList for document summarization is interesting.** The AI presentation tech had value outside presentations — the underlying AI formatting/layout engine. StartSuite should think about where its brand intelligence engine has value beyond the core product.
- **Lightfield pivoting to sales intelligence suggests the founders saw more opportunity in intelligence than in content creation.** This is directionally aligned with StartSuite's thesis that intelligence (context) is more valuable than output (assets).

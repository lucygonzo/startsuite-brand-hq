# DesignJoy — Competitive Profile

> Category: Direct Competitor
> Last researched: March 26, 2026
> Threat level: Low
> Primary tier impact: Day-Ones, Scalers
> Sources: designjoy.co

---

## Company Overview

- Founded 2017 by Brett Williams; solo operator
- Team: 1 person (Brett Williams does all design work)
- Revenue: $130K+ MRR, $1.7M+ ARR
- 40+ active clients at any given time
- No external funding — fully bootstrapped

## Product & Pricing

- Unlimited design subscription, async delivery via Trello
- Standard: $4,995/mo — 1 active request at a time, avg 48hr turnaround
- Pro: $7,995/mo — 2 active requests at a time
- Webflow development: $999/mo add-on
- No contracts, pause or cancel anytime
- Waitlist model — demand exceeds capacity

## Target Audience & GTM

- Startups and tech companies that need fast, high-quality design without hiring
- Clients: Beehiiv, AngelList, Mercury
- Homepage headline: "Your personal design team"
- GTM: Almost entirely word of mouth + Twitter/X personal brand
- Brett's personal following drives all demand — no paid acquisition, no sales team
- Waitlist creates scarcity and social proof

## How They Position

- "Your personal design team" — intimacy and exclusivity
- Positions against hiring a full-time designer (cost, speed, quality)
- Solo operator is the brand — Brett's taste and speed are the product
- Anti-agency positioning: no account managers, no overhead, just design
- Key claim: senior-quality design at a fraction of hiring cost

## Threat Assessment

- **Threat level:** Low
- **Tiers impacted:** Day-Ones and Scalers who need design output and might choose DesignJoy over StartSuite for pure execution speed
- **Their strongest argument:** Proven solo model at $1.7M ARR. Fast turnaround. No bloat.
- **StartSuite's strongest argument:** Full creative operating system vs design-only. StartSuite delivers strategy, brand intelligence, competitive monitoring, content, and copy — not just visual design. Context compounds.
- **Trending toward our positioning?** No — Brett has stayed firmly in execution-only territory. No strategy, no intelligence, no expansion beyond design.
- **Biggest vulnerability:** One person = hard capacity ceiling. No strategy layer. No content or copy. No brand intelligence. If Brett burns out or steps away, the business evaporates. Cannot serve clients who need more than design files.

## What StartSuite Can Learn

- The waitlist/scarcity model is powerful for demand generation. StartSuite could use capacity-based scarcity for Dream Client engagements.
- Brett's personal brand IS the business. Lucy's personal brand should be a stronger acquisition channel for StartSuite.
- The "pause anytime" flexibility removes friction. StartSuite's Foundations membership could benefit from a pause option rather than cancel-only.
- His Twitter/X presence converts directly to revenue. Study his content cadence and topics.

## Key Quotes / Copy to Watch

- "Your personal design team"
- The solo-operator narrative is aspirational for founders — watch if he expands or stays solo
- His public revenue transparency builds trust and attracts press

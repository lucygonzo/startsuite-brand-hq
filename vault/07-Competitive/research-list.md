## Research Template (for each company)

When deep dives begin, each competitor profile should include:
- Company overview (founded, HQ, team size, funding)
- Pricing model and tiers
- Target audience
- Positioning and tagline
- Notable clients
- LinkedIn followers and content strategy
- Glassdoor/employee sentiment
- Recent news, funding, or pivots
- Threat level to StartSuite (High/Medium/Low/Adjacent)
- Vulnerabilities
- StartSuite's specific advantage
- What we can learn from them

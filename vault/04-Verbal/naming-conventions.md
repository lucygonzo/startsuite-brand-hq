# StartSuite Naming Conventions

> Last updated: March 26, 2026
> Source: `brandData.ts` — `verbalIdentity.namingConventions`

---

## Rules

1. **StartSuite** — Always write 'StartSuite' as one word with capital S and capital S. Never 'Start Suite' or 'startsuite'.

2. **Tagline** — The tagline is 'Creative Operating System for Founders and Growing Companies'. Capitalize all major words.

3. **Model name** — Refer to the model as 'creative operating system'. Never 'agency', 'vendor', or 'creative infrastructure'.

4. **Clients** — Clients are 'founders', 'teams', or 'companies' in external-facing copy. Never 'customers' or 'clients'.

5. **46Capital** — The backer is '46Capital' as one word, no space. Never '46 Capital' in logo contexts.

6. **Brand Discovery** — The core product is 'Brand Discovery' (capitalized). It is the entry point and North Star.

7. **Foundations / Brand HQ** — The subscription is 'Foundations' or 'Brand HQ'. These are interchangeable in client-facing contexts.

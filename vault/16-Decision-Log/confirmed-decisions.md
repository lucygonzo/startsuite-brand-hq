| **Owner** | Reagan + Lucy |
| **Date** | March 2026 |
| **Status** | Confirmed |

**Context:** Every piece of content must pass the test: "Would an expert share this?" If it wouldn't pass muster with a senior brand strategist, it doesn't get published.

### D10: AI-Empowered, Not AI-Led

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Owner** | Reagan + Lucy |
| **Date** | March 2026 |
| **Status** | Confirmed |

**Context:** Senior creative judgment leads every engagement. AI accelerates research, content production, and competitive analysis. StartSuite is not positioned as an "AI company" even though AI powers everything behind the scenes.

**Rationale:** Positioning as AI-led would commoditize the offering. The value is human judgment and taste, accelerated by AI. This attracts clients who want a partner, not a tool.

# Paid Media Strategy

**Last Updated:** March 26, 2026
**Source:** brandData.ts digital.paidMedia

---

## Key Takeaway

Paid media is Phase 2. Organic content on LinkedIn + YouTube builds the foundation first. When paid launches, it is LinkedIn-first targeting founders and marketing leaders by job title and company stage.

---

## Channels

### LinkedIn Ads

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| Status | Phase 2 |
| Objective | Awareness + Lead Gen |
| Targeting | Founders (CEO/Co-Founder), Marketing Directors/CMOs, VC Partners — company size 1-50 |
| Investment | $1-3K/month |

**Formats:**
- Thought leadership posts (boosted)
- Lead Gen Forms
- Sponsored InMail for warm audiences

**Notes:** Start with boosting organic posts that perform well. LinkedIn is the highest-intent channel for B2B founder audiences.

---

### Google Search

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| Status | Phase 2 |
| Objective | High-intent capture |
| Targeting | Keywords: "brand strategy for startups", "startup brand consultant", "brand operating system", "brand intelligence platform" |
| Investment | $500-1K/month |

**Formats:**
- Search ads
- Responsive display

**Notes:** Capture founders and marketing leaders actively searching for solutions. Lower volume than LinkedIn but higher purchase intent.

| Phase | Channel | Status |

## Pre-Requisites for Phase 2 Launch

Before activating paid media:

1. LinkedIn organic content cadence at 3x/week is consistent
2. At least 2-3 published case studies for landing page proof
3. Brand Discovery pricing is finalized and published
4. Website conversion flow is optimized (CTA to Brand Discovery call)
5. Tracking and attribution infrastructure is in place

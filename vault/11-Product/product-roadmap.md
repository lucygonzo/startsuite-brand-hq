# Product Roadmap

**Last Updated:** March 26, 2026
**Source:** brandData.product.roadmap (brandData.ts)

---

## Key Takeaway

StartSuite's product model is a pipeline: Brand Discovery (entry point) flows to Foundations (core subscription) flows to Add-ons (modular expansion) flows to Dream Client (full-service). The March 2026 pivot positions Brand HQ as a living creative intelligence platform that compounds context over time.

---

## Phase 1: Current (Q1-Q2 2026)

**Status:** Active
**Focus:** Services + Brand HQ manual build -- prove the creative operating system model

**Items:**
- Brand Discovery productized as standalone premium offering
- Foundations at $3K/month with clear scope
- Brand HQ built manually for each client with deep research
- First 3 case studies published
- n8n automation infrastructure for competitive research
- Brand HQ template system for repeatable delivery
- First accelerator partnership
- First hire: creative generalist with AI tool fluency

---

## Phase 2: Semi-Automation (Q3-Q4 2026)

**Status:** Planned
**Focus:** Semi-automated Brand HQ, client self-serve features, content creation tools

**Items:**
- Semi-automated Brand HQ build process reducing delivery time
- Client self-serve features: content generation from Brand HQ data
- Competitive monitoring dashboard for Foundations subscribers
- Token/credit model for add-on services launched
- YouTube channel active with monthly shoot day content
- 10+ active Foundations subscribers
- 2+ accelerator partnerships
- Second hire consideration based on demand

---

## Phase 3: Full Platform (2027)

**Status:** Vision
**Focus:** Full platform with compliance checker, automated competitive monitoring, creative review workflow

**Items:**
- Brand compliance checker -- automated review of assets against Brand HQ guidelines
- Automated competitive monitoring -- real-time alerts when competitors make moves
- Creative review workflow -- submit work for Brand HQ-informed feedback
- 25+ active Foundations subscribers
- Dream Client model proven with 2-3 full-service engagements
- National accelerator partnerships
- Team of 4-5 with specialized roles

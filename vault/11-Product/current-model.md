# Current Product Model

**Last Updated:** March 26, 2026
**Source:** brandData.product.currentModel (brandData.ts)

## Model Type

Creative Operating System -- Pipeline Product Model

## Description

Brand Discovery -> Foundations -> Add-ons -> Dream Client. Each tier builds on the last. Brand Discovery produces the Brand HQ. Foundations keeps it alive. Add-ons extend it. Dream Client is the full-service commitment.

---

## Product Phases

### 1. Brand Discovery

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Duration** | 2-4 weeks |
| **Deliverables** | Living Brand HQ, competitive landscape, audience mapping, brand strategy foundation |
| **Pricing** | Premium standalone (TBD -- options: $5K flat, $3-5K range based on scope, or included in first month) |

### 2. Foundations (Brand HQ)

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Duration** | Ongoing monthly |
| **Deliverables** | Context management, competitive monitoring, monthly state-of-the-union, office hours, strategy sessions, decision audit log, AI resources |
| **Pricing** | $3,000/month |

### 3. Add-On Services

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Duration** | As needed |
| **Deliverables** | Social content strategy, paid ads, web design/dev, AI automation, brand refresh/rebrand |
| **Pricing** | Modular pricing per engagement (token/credit model under consideration) |

### 4. Dream Client

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Duration** | Ongoing monthly |
| **Deliverables** | Everything included -- full-service creative operating system with dedicated partnership |
| **Pricing** | $10K+/month (custom, may include equity/revenue-share) |

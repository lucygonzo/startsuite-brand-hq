# Future Features and Platform Vision

**Last Updated:** March 26, 2026
**Source:** brandData.product.paasVision, brandData.product.differentiators, March 2026 board meeting (brandData.ts)

---

## Platform-as-a-Service Vision

**Description:** Brand HQ evolves from a manually-built living document into a creative intelligence platform. The near-term is high-touch, senior-led engagements that prove the model. The mid-term introduces semi-automation and client self-serve features. The long-term is a full platform with compliance checking, automated competitive monitoring, and creative review workflows.

**Core Principle:** Prove the model with people first. Automate when the patterns are clear. Never let AI replace judgment.

**Target User:** Current state: all clients start with Brand Discovery and graduate to Foundations. Future state: companies can access Brand HQ intelligence with varying levels of service, from self-serve to full-service.

---

## Key Capabilities (Current and Planned)

1. Brand HQ as a living intelligence platform (current -- custom-built for each client)
2. Competitive landscape research automation (in progress -- n8n workflows)
3. AI resource library accessible to Foundations subscribers
4. Client self-serve content generation from Brand HQ data (future Phase 2)
5. Automated competitive monitoring dashboard (future Phase 2)
6. Brand compliance checker (future Phase 3)
7. Creative review workflow (future Phase 3)

---

## Future Features from Board Meeting

### Brand Compliance Checker (Phase 3)
Automated review of assets against Brand HQ guidelines. Ensures that any content or creative produced by the client team or external partners aligns with the brand standards documented in Brand HQ.

### Self-Serve Content Creation (Phase 2)
Client self-serve features that allow teams to generate content from Brand HQ data. This reduces manual work while increasing value perception and client autonomy.

### Creative Review Workflow (Phase 3)
Submit work for Brand HQ-informed feedback. A workflow where clients or their teams can submit creative assets for automated and human review against brand guidelines.

## Differentiators

- Brand Discovery as a standalone premium product -- no competitor offers this as a gateway
- Living Brand HQ that compounds intelligence over time -- not static deliverables
- AI-empowered research and competitive analysis -- not AI-generated creative
- 46Capital relationship provides deal flow and investor-perspective validation
- Empowerment model: clients become better brand managers, not more dependent
- Senior creative judgment with AI acceleration -- taste at scale

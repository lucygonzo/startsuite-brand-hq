# StartSuite Product Set

**Last Updated:** March 26, 2026
**Source:** brandData.ts services array, project_product_pricing.md (March 2026 board meeting)

---

## Overview

StartSuite operates a pipeline product model: Brand Discovery (entry point) flows to Foundations (core subscription) flows to Add-ons (modular expansion) flows to Dream Client (full-service). Each tier builds on the last.

---

## Tier 1: Brand Discovery

**Subtitle:** Standalone Premium Entry Point
**Phase:** Entry Point
**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks
**Pricing:** Premium standalone (TBD — options: $5K flat, $3-5K range based on scope, or included in first month of Foundations)

**Description:** Deep research into your brand, audience, competitive landscape, and market position. No templates. No shortcuts. This is the North Star product that produces the living Brand HQ intelligence layer. Can be sold standalone as a premium product or as the first step toward Foundations.

### Deliverables

- Complete Brand HQ (living strategic intelligence document)
- Competitive landscape research and positioning matrix
- Audience segmentation and persona development
- Brand identity audit and strategic recommendations
- Verbal identity framework and messaging hierarchy
- Go-to-market strategy foundation
- Decision audit log initialization

### Strategic Notes

- Brand Discovery is the entry point for every engagement — it is both a product and the most powerful sales tool
- Surfaces the client's North Star: how they want to be perceived, who they're talking to, what they do better than anyone
- Produces structured brand documentation (Markdown files, brand guide, verbal/visual identity)
- Creates the foundation for Brand HQ and all AI-assisted workflows

---

## Tier 2: Foundations (Brand HQ)

**Subtitle:** $3,000/month Retainer
**Phase:** Ongoing
**Pricing:** $3,000/month (up from original $2,000 target, confirmed March 2026)

**Description:** The ongoing creative operating system. Context management, competitive monitoring, monthly brand state-of-the-union reports, office hours, and strategy sessions. The system gets smarter every month.

### What Is Included

- Brand HQ context management and monthly updates
- Competitive monitoring and landscape evolution tracking
- Monthly brand state-of-the-union report
- Office hours and strategy sessions
- Decision audit log maintenance
- AI resource access and personalized brand tools
- Weekly reports with TL;DR + option to dig deeper
- Monthly 1-hour strategy session reviewing trends, performance, pivots

### What Is NOT Included

- Recording/producing original content
- Ads management
- Full website builds
- Content creation, filming, community management

### Pricing Notes

- Token/credit option discussed for anti-retainer clients (credits don't expire, roll over)
- Decision audit log includes context and rationale for every brand decision

---

## Tier 3: Add-On Services

**Subtitle:** Modular, Per Engagement
**Phase:** Modular
**Pricing:** Per engagement (token/credit model under consideration)

**Description:** Modular services that layer on top of Foundations. Each add-on is priced per engagement and informed by the Brand HQ intelligence layer.

### Deliverables

- Social content strategy and LinkedIn thought leadership
- Paid advertising management (LinkedIn Ads, Google Search)
- Website design and development (positioning-led, not brochure) — $5K starting point
- AI automation setup (n8n workflows, brand-aware pipelines)
- Brand refresh or full rebrand execution

### Notes

- Social media content creation and management (NOT community management)
- Ad creative and paid media support (future hire needed — Lucy does not want to run ads)
- AI automation setup and maintenance

---

## Tier 4: Dream Client (Full-Service)

**Subtitle:** Custom Engagement — $10K+/month
**Phase:** Custom
**Pricing:** Custom pricing with upfront setup fee; retainer scales with client revenue; equity or revenue-share in select cases

**Description:** Everything. Full-service creative operating system for select clients. Brand management, content creation, paid media, web development, AI automation, and dedicated strategic partnership. Example: Steadwell (peptides).

### Deliverables

- Everything in Foundations
- All add-on services included
- Dedicated strategic partnership
- Custom pricing and scope
- Equity/revenue-share consideration for right-fit clients

## How It Works

| Step | Title | Description | Label |
|------|-------|-------------|-------|
| 1 | We start with Brand Discovery. | Deep research into your brand, audience, competitors, and market position. No templates. No shortcuts. The Brand HQ intelligence layer is built here. | Intelligence |
| 2 | Your Brand HQ goes live. | A living strategic document that houses all your brand intelligence. Updated monthly. The single source of truth for every creative decision. | Foundation |
| 3 | Context compounds every month. | Monthly state-of-the-union reports, competitive monitoring, strategy sessions. The system gets smarter the longer you use it. | Operating System |
| 4 | You're empowered, not dependent. | We teach you to use your brand, not gatekeep it. AI-powered resources, guides, and tools personalized to your Brand HQ. | Empowerment |

## Revenue Mix Targets

- ~50% Foundations-only clients (brand intelligence, no execution)
- ~25-30% larger full-service clients (outlier/dream client model)
- ~20-25% Foundations-plus-content clients

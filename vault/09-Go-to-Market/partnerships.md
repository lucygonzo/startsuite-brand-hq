# Partnership Tiers

**Last Updated:** March 26, 2026
**Source:** brandData.digital.partnerships (brandData.ts)

## Key Takeaway

Partnerships are the highest-leverage distribution channel. Each accelerator partnership unlocks a cohort of pre-qualified companies. The referral network generates warm inbound at zero cost.

---

## Anchor Partner

**Description:** 46Capital -- the founding backer and primary deal flow source. Every 46Capital portfolio company is a warm StartSuite prospect.

**Examples:**
- 46Capital portfolio companies
- 46Capital fund LPs with portfolio

**Status:** Active

## Accelerator Partners

**Description:** Accelerators that embed StartSuite as the creative operating system for their cohort companies. Brand Discovery as a cohort benefit. Demo day prep as the highest-value entry point.

**Examples:**
- Tulsa Innovation Labs
- Heartland-based accelerators
- National accelerators with Heartland cohorts

**Status:** Target -- First partnership is a priority action item

---

## Referral Network

**Description:** Attorneys, accountants, fractional executives, and other professional services that serve growing companies and refer clients who need brand work.

**Examples:**
- Startup attorneys
- Fractional CFOs
- Startup accountants
- Fractional CMOs

**Status:** Target -- Referral program needs formalization

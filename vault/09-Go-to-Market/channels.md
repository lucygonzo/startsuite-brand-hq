## Phase 1 Channels (Active / Launching)

### Brand Discovery (Direct Sales)
- **Investment:** Sales conversations, proposals
- **Status:** Phase 1 -- Active
- **Notes:** The primary direct acquisition channel. Every prospect starts with a Brand Discovery conversation.

### 46Capital Portfolio Referrals
- **Investment:** Relationship maintenance
- **Status:** Phase 1 -- Active
- **Notes:** Warm pipeline from the founding backer. Every 46Capital portfolio company is a natural StartSuite prospect.

### LinkedIn Content (Reagan + Lucy)
- **Investment:** 3x/week each + monthly shoot day
- **Status:** Phase 1 -- Launching
- **Notes:** Personal brand content from Reagan and Lucy as primary distribution. Expert insight standard applies to every post.

### YouTube Content
- **Investment:** 2-4x/month from shoot day material
- **Status:** Phase 1 -- Launching
- **Notes:** Long-form authority content from monthly shoot day. War room internal + expert guest interviews.

### Accelerator Partnerships
- **Investment:** Partnership development
- **Status:** Phase 1 -- Targeting first
- **Notes:** Each partnership unlocks 10-20 pre-qualified companies per year. First partnership is a priority action item.

### Referral Program
- **Investment:** Formalization needed
- **Status:** Phase 1 -- Planning
- **Notes:** Referral mechanism, incentive structure, and tracking system need to be built. Target: 1-2 referrals per active client per year.

# Funnel Audits

**Last Updated:** March 26, 2026
**Source:** Derived from brandData.journey, brandData.gapDimensions, brandData.gtm (brandData.ts)

---

## Overview

Funnel audits map conversion friction across each audience segment at every stage of the client journey. Data is derived from the journey stages, gap dimensions, and GTM analysis in brandData.

---

## Segment: Early-Stage Startups (Pre-Seed to Seed)

| Stage | Friction | Severity |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Awareness | Not yet known outside 46Capital ecosystem; organic discovery is minimal | High |
| Consideration | No published case studies; Brand Discovery pricing not visible | High |
| Decision | Budget objection from resource-constrained founders; category confusion ("what is a creative OS?") | Medium |
| Onboarding | Founders are time-constrained; need async-first process to reduce friction | Low |
| Retention | Compound value needs to be made visible monthly; founders may deprioritize brand | Medium |
| Advocacy | No formal referral mechanism; founders are willing to refer but need a clear process | Low |

---

## Segment: Growing Companies with Brand Debt (Series A+)

| Stage | Friction | Severity |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Awareness | Limited reach beyond VC ecosystem; LinkedIn cadence not yet consistent | High |
| Consideration | No comparison content (OS vs. agency vs. freelancer); no Brand HQ demo available | Medium |
| Decision | Previous agency burn creates skepticism; need proof of different model | Medium |
| Onboarding | Complex brand architecture (multiple sub-brands) requires more discovery time | Medium |
| Retention | Scope creep risk if inclusions/exclusions not clearly enforced | Medium |
| Advocacy | Discretion principle limits public case studies for some clients | Low |

---

## Segment: Mid-Stage with New Leadership (CMOs, Directors)

| Stage | Friction | Severity |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Awareness | Not yet recognized as a thought leader in the CMO/marketing director community | High |
| Consideration | Anti-retainer mindset creates resistance to monthly pricing model | High |
| Decision | Need to justify investment to CEO with data; ROI framing not yet strong enough | Medium |
| Onboarding | Need fast results to prove internal credibility within 90 days | Medium |
| Retention | Token/credit model not yet implemented; monthly retainer may not fit | High |
| Advocacy | High potential for referrals within marketing leader networks if satisfied | Low |

---

## Segment: Enterprise Brand Teams

| Stage | Friction | Severity |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Awareness | No presence in enterprise marketing channels; relies on personal referrals | High |
| Consideration | No enterprise-specific case studies or references | High |
| Decision | Procurement processes and budget approval cycles are longer | Medium |
| Onboarding | Larger teams require more stakeholder alignment | Medium |
| Retention | Need to demonstrate value to multiple internal stakeholders | Medium |
| Advocacy | Enterprise referrals are high-value but slow-moving | Low |

## Cross-Segment Funnel Gaps (Summary)

| Gap | Impact | Priority |
|-----|--------|----------|
| No published Brand Discovery pricing | Blocks self-qualification at Consideration stage | High |
| No published case studies | Blocks trust-building at Consideration stage | High |
| LinkedIn cadence not yet at 3x/week | Limits top-of-funnel awareness | High |
| No token/credit model for add-ons | Loses anti-retainer buyers at Decision stage | Medium |
| No formal referral program | Misses low-cost acquisition at Advocacy stage | Medium |
| No Brand HQ demo or walkthrough | Reduces Consideration-to-Decision conversion | Medium |

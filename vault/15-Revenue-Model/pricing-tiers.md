## Brand Discovery

**Price:** TBD ($5K flat / $3-5K range)
**Current Mix:** 20% | **Target Mix:** 30%

**Strengths:**
- Repeatable process with high margin
- Standalone premium product
- Best pipeline tool for Foundations conversion
- Demonstrates creative operating system value upfront

**Gaps:**
- Not yet priced -- blocks the sales pipeline
- Prospects can't self-qualify without a price anchor
- Pricing tension: premium signal vs. accessible gateway

---

## Foundations (Brand HQ)

**Price:** $3,000/month
**Current Mix:** 50% | **Target Mix:** 40%

**Strengths:**
- Recurring revenue with predictable cash flow
- Compounds context over time -- natural retention
- Clear scope prevents scope creep
- High retention when compound value is visible

**Gaps:**
- Churn risk if monthly value is not demonstrated visibly
- Requires consistent monthly state-of-the-union delivery
- Anti-retainer buyers may resist monthly commitment

---

## Add-On Services

**Price:** Variable per engagement
**Current Mix:** 10% | **Target Mix:** 20%

**Strengths:**
- High margin on content, ads, and web projects
- Leverages Brand HQ context for better delivery
- Modular flexibility for clients
- Expands revenue per client without new acquisition

**Gaps:**
- Capacity limited by team size
- Token/credit model not yet implemented
- Anti-retainer buyers need flexible purchasing mechanism

---

## Dream Client (Full-Service)

**Price:** $10K+/month (custom)
**Current Mix:** 20% | **Target Mix:** 10%

**Strengths:**
- Deepest client relationship
- Highest revenue per client
- Equity/revenue-share upside potential
- Proves the full-service creative operating system model

**Gaps:**
- Not scalable -- outlier model
- Each Dream Client absorbs significant senior capacity
- Custom pricing makes pipeline forecasting harder

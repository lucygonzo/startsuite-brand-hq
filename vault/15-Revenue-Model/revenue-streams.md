## Target Revenue Mix

The revenue model targets a balanced mix that prioritizes recurring Foundations revenue while growing Brand Discovery as a pipeline driver and expanding Add-on services for per-client revenue growth.

| Stream | Current Mix | Target Mix | Direction |
|--------|-------------|------------|-----------|
| Brand Discovery | 20% | 30% | Increase -- productize as standalone premium |
| Foundations (Brand HQ) | 50% | 40% | Core revenue -- maintain as largest stream |
| Add-On Services | 10% | 20% | Grow -- modular expansion per client |
| Dream Client | 20% | 10% | Reduce share -- not scalable, keep as outlier |

---

## Revenue Mix Strategy

### Foundations: 50% current -> 40% target
Foundations at $3,000/month is the core recurring revenue engine. Target is to maintain it as the largest revenue stream while reducing over-reliance. Each Foundations subscriber represents $36K ARR. Target: 10+ subscribers by end of 2026, 25+ by 2027.

### Brand Discovery: 20% current -> 30% target
Brand Discovery is being elevated from a pre-engagement step to a standalone premium product. Pricing under consideration ($5K flat, $3-5K range, or included in first month of Foundations). Each completed Brand Discovery feeds the Foundations pipeline.

### Add-On Services: 10% current -> 20% target
Add-on services (social content strategy, paid ads, web design/dev, AI automation, brand refresh) are modular and high-margin. Growing this stream requires:
- Token/credit model implementation for anti-retainer buyers
- Clear add-on service menu with pricing
- Capacity expansion through first hire

### Dream Client: 20% current -> 10% target
Dream Client engagements at $10K+/month (custom, equity/revenue-share possible) are the highest revenue per client but consume the most senior capacity. The target is to keep 2-3 Dream Clients active while scaling through Foundations and Add-ons.
